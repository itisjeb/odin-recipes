Stuffed Potatoes

Piping hot twice baked stuffed potatoes - serves 3-4

Ingredients

  • 6 agria potatoes
  • 6 bacon slices
  • 2 cups cheese, shredded
  • 50g butter
  • Sour cream - to taste
  • Salt, pepper - to taste
  • Spring onions - to taste
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees celsius and line an oven tray
  2. Prick potatoes all over with a fork
  3. Coat potatoes in olive oil, and season with salt and pepper
  4. Place potatoes on prepared oven tray, and when oven is hot, place inside
  5. Bake potatoes for 60-90 minutes depending on size of potato
  6. While potatoes are baking, fry bacon in pan and cut into bite sized pieces
  7. After an hour of baking time, pull potatoes from oven and test for doneness using a knife
  8. Remove potatoes for oven - but leave the oven turned on
  9. Cut the potatoes in half. Careful, they're hot!
  10. Using a spoon, scoop the inside of the potatoes out into a bowl
  11. Add to bowl butter, cheese - reserving some for the final bake, oven, and salt/pepper to taste
  12. Mix until it all comes into a delicious, smooth, potatoey good time
  13. Scoop back into your empty potato skins
  14. Top stuffed potatoes with remaining cheese
  15. Pop potatoes back into the oven until they are hot and the top is crispy
  16. Serve with sour cream and spring onions, as much as you'd like!
