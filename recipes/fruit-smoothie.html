Fruit Smoothie

Refreshing fruit smoothie - serves 2

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 2 cups frozen fruit of your choice
  • 500ml milk (dairy, almond, whatever floats your boat)
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

Steps

  1. Into your blender, add all ingredients
  2. Blend until smooth! You may need to add more milk depending on type of frozen fruit added
  3. Serve in your favourite glass
